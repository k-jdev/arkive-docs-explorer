# $ARK Token

$ARK is the native token of the Arkive protocol. Its role is deliberately narrow. A portion of all revenue, from both subscriptions and practice fees, is directed to buying $ARK on the open market and burning it. $ARK has a fixed supply of 1,000,000 tokens with no transfer tax, and its initial liquidity is seeded on Ethereum, with additional pools opened on other chains over time.
